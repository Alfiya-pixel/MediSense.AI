Our Story

MediSense began with a straightforward observation: navigating healthcare is hard. Symptoms overlap, medical jargon is complex, and appointments aren't always immediately available. We set out to bridge that gap with technology.

Our team of healthcare professionals, data scientists, and engineers came together to build a platform that translates raw symptoms into actionable, understandable health insights — powered by a Support Vector Machine model trained on extensive medical datasets.

We're not replacing doctors. We're empowering you with knowledge so that when you do see a professional, you're informed, prepared, and confident.
