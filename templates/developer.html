About Alfiya Asma

Hello! I'm Alfiya Asma — an AI and Machine Learning Engineer turning complex data into meaningful, user-centric applications. My journey started with a deep fascination for how algorithms can simulate human reasoning, and that curiosity has only grown over the years.

My work is driven by a commitment to excellence and a belief that technology should be accessible and beneficial to everyone. Whether it's developing predictive models, creating intelligent algorithms, or designing clean user interfaces — I strive for solutions that are both cutting-edge and practical.

MediSense represents the convergence of two things I care about deeply: AI capability and healthcare accessibility. I believe intelligent systems can meaningfully improve how people understand and respond to their health — and this project is a step in that direction.
